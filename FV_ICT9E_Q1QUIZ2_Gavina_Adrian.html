Moana


The main character, Moana. Is an adventorous teenager that wants to explore the Ocean all by herself.
Although her parents doesn't allow her, she did not listen to them and she still explored the Ocean. Because she
wants to save her people. Their Island is slowly dying, thier food supplies are dwindling. She takes herself to
find the solution. During her journey,Moana meets Maui, a once-mighty demigod. He guides her to complete her quest of
becoming a master way-finder. Together, they sail across the Ocean to find the heart of Te Fiti to restore the peace and prevent the Darkness from spreading.
